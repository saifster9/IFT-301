About Us

Welcome to the Arizona State University Bike Co-op, where cycling meets community! We're here to support your biking journey with a range of services and resources. Whether you're a seasoned rider or just getting started, we've got something for everyone.

Services

We provide a variety of services to help you keep on cycling. All our services are free to currently registered ASU students.

Free Bike Repair

Got a flat tire or a squeaky chain? Our experienced mechanics are here to help! Take advantage of our free bike repair services and get your wheels rolling smoothly again.

DIY Repair Workshops

Empower yourself with knowledge! Join our hands-on workshops to learn how to handle basic bike repairs on your own. From fixing flats to adjusting gears, we've got you covered.

Free Tool Rental

Need a specialized tool for a quick tune-up? Borrow one from our extensive tool collection at no cost. We believe everyone should have access to the tools they need for a well-maintained ride.

Bicycle Rental

Don't have a bike? No problem! Rent one of our well-maintained bicycles and explore the beautiful surroundings near the Mesa-Gateway airport. Whether you're commuting or cruising, we have the perfect ride for you.

Traffic Safety Classes

Our experienced instructors cover essential topics such as road etiquette, signal usage, and effective communication with motorists. These classes go beyond the basics, delving into strategies for handling various traffic scenarios and ensuring a safe and enjoyable cycling experience.

Organized Rides

Join us for our next organized ride and discover the scenic routes around the Mesa-Gateway airport area. It's a fantastic opportunity to connect with fellow cyclists and explore the beauty of Arizona on two wheels.
